## Executive Summary

This 5-day crash course introduces beginners to Python for data science, focusing on **data wrangling, statistical analysis, and visualisation** using high-quality free resources.

Primary sources:

- https://wesmckinney.com/book/ (Python for Data Analysis)
- https://jakevdp.github.io/PythonDataScienceHandbook/
- https://pandas.pydata.org/docs/user_guide/index.html
