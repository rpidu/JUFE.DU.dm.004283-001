# JUFE HDa - Data Management (004283-001)

---

## Summary

**jufe.du.dm.004283-001** is a JUFE and HDa joint course on fundamental data management - in the data science discipline - focused on beginner level developers of scientific digital tools.

This course equips students with theoretical understanding and practical skills in scientific inquiry, data handling, and computational analysis. Through an interdisciplinary and research-oriented approach, students will engage in both conceptual and applied aspects of data management.

### Main Objectives

- Design and execute [experimental studies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Experiment) and [observational studies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Observational_study)
- Use [scripting languages](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scripting_language) to apply [statistical data analysis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Statistical_analysis)
- Explain [statistical methods](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Statistics) targeting [scientific inquiries](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific_method)
- Relate and understand [theories of science](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philosophy_of_science)
- Apply [academic writing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Academic_writing) and the [scientific method](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific_method)

---

## Course Structure

- Duration: **4 weeks**
- Weekly Load: **5 lectures per week**, 3-4 in last week
- Total Lectures: **18-19** lectures

---

## Weekly Themes

- **Week 1:** The method - [science](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific_method) and the [collegium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Learned_society)
- **Week 2:** The [script](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scripting_language) - data and [file formats](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File_format)
- **Week 3:** The [data](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data) - [systems modeling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mathematical_model) and [data collection](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data_collection)
- **Week 4:** The [review](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scholarly_peer_review) - [project execution](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Project_management) and [results presentation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Presentation)

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, students will:

- Demonstrate competence in designing scientific studies
- Apply scripting techniques for data analysis
- Interpret and explain statistical findings
- Connect practical work to philosophical scientific frameworks
- Produce structured academic reports and presentations
