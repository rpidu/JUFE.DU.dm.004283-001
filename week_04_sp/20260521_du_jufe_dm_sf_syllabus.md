# Synthesis and Forum (3-4 Days)

## Executive Summary

This 3-day part of the course leave room for reflection and presentation of the insights, gained revolving around the groups work on projects during the course; it is the synthesis and conclusion of each groups project results and analysis, as well as the final week and presentation of the course.

This week is fully **group work** and all results go into the **group repository**; _real data_ collected from _individuals_ should **not be published** in the public repository, or online elsewhere, during this course.

---

## Prioritised Syllabus

| Day | Focus        | Outcome                                   |
| --- | ------------ | ----------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Synthesis    | Render insights from results and analysis |
| 2   | Summary      | Produce a minimal abstract of the work    |
| (3) | Preparations | Extra time to prepare presentations       |
| 3/4 | Forum        | Presentations and academic review         |

Each day, the students organise in their project groups, working together along the workflow already established individually - as previously done during week 02.

- Start detailing the work that need to be done
  - discuss design together in group and with AI jointly
    - use eligable work targets from week 02, that match and correspond to the tasks for the day
  - split work between peers in the group, if nescessary to work separately with AI in parallell
- Discuss and summarise work into the markdown formatted IMRAD report in the **group repository**
  - together with resulting protocols from AI discussions, and
  - relevant generated code
- For each day, document with a protocol in `.md` format, what you have discussed
  - within the group
  - with AI in the group
  - separately with AI
    - if necessary, work in branches which you merge into the default branch, once done.

### 1. Synthesis (Day 1)

The groups continues with the discussion, answering their research questions the best day can, and reflecting on the implemented methodolog. Weaknesses and strengths, of the method and assessed data, is openly and clearly discussed. The logical conclusion is rendered and a very short remark on further work is made.

- The resulting summaries from this days work is expected to go into the end of the Discussion section of the IMRaD report.

### 2. Summary (Day 2)

A one paragraph long summary is produced, consisting of 3-5 sentences; write the motivation to the project, ie. the impact, detail the challenge, providing your methodology as approach. End with your results and any hints on further work. Summarise the whole abstract in the first sentence of the paragraph.

- This abstract comes directly after the reports title in the IMRaD-style report.

### 3. Forum (Day 3 or 4)

The students present their work and answer a set of questions, related to their work, in an open forum in a class room setting.

- Each fora starts with the full class present
- The group presents 5 minutes using ex. presentation slides, sharing the time and material equally within the group
  - if using presentation slides, 1-2 slides per student is a good target
- The rest of the class, ie. the forum, asks questions in 5-10 minutes to the presenting group
  - the questions are shared equally between the students in the presenting group
